About Me
I have been a Business Intelligence technologist for over 20 years, starting my IT career while working for NCNB in Greensboro NC. Previous to that, my educational background included a BS in Economics from Va Tech and my MBA from UNC-G. Other, not technical positions included Store Manager at a Domino's Pizza and later collections for banks in Va and NC.

Since beginning my IT career with Information Builders in 1997, I have become proficient in their software, WebFOCUS. As a consultant in their Professional Services group, I have worked on projects large and small for all manner of organizations, business and government. As a consultant, we were epected to also be proficient in rendering technologies including HTML, CSS and javascript.

My prefernce, though, are back end technologies which cull data from throughout the organization and stage it for quick retrievals through Web enabled reporting solutions. A great number of my past experiences, starting with my first job at NCNB, revolved around the accumulation of data from disparate sources and putting them into a unified structure which enabled quick reporting.
